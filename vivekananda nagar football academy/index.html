About Us

Vivekananda Yuva Football Academy

Founded on the ideals of Swami Vivekananda, our academy is committed to nurturing the next generation of football talent. We empower young athletes through structured training, guided by experienced coaches.

With personal attention to every player and a strong focus on discipline, teamwork, and values — we are building more than just players; we are shaping champions in life and in the game.

  • ⚽ Intermediate Academy (ages 11–14)
  • ⚽ Elite Development Program (ages 15–18)
  • ⚽ Summer Camps & Clinics
